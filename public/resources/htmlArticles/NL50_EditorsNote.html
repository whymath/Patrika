Editors’ Note

 

Hello, OBP!

 

We are glad to bring to you our 50th issue of the Newsletter, and we, have the good fortune of being able to do this around the time that we turn 2 years old. For all of us at the editorial team, the past year has been an amazing experience, and publishing this milestone issue is a proud and exciting moment for us.

 

We have come quite a long way from our humble beginnings in 2013, when Madhur had come up with the idea to start a publication ‘of the team, by the team, and for the team’ to improve the internal communication and collaboration. His foresight, vision and guidance are the main reasons that we are still going strong today, two years and fifty issues after our “Origination”. We’d sincerely like to thank him, as well as our contributors, supporters and well-wishers; those who have stayed with us all this time and those that those who joined us along the way. We literally could not have done this without all of you.

 

The last year has been a delightful run, with the size of our editorial team more than doubling to 12 people (from 5 the same time last year), and a lot of old and new faces collaborating with us and helping us deliver a wide variety of content. Similar to last year, we’re celebrating this anniversary with some snapshots of the stories published and memories created during our second year in this issue below, including introductions to our team and some of our most frequent contributors. Towards this end, we had sent out a small teaser mail last week to this issue that included a small voting exercise for the sections of the Newsletter you liked best. We have the results for you here:

 

 

Looking ahead, along with all the hustle and the dynamic changes happening with our projects, quite a few things are changing for us at the Newsletter too. As of this issue, our reader-base is expanding to a wider audience that includes teams outside India and Australia, and we are planning to get new faces onboard for contributing, designing and editing, while at the same time increasing the scope and improving the quality of our delivery. This initiative that began 2 years ago isn’t over, and we definitely need help from all of you to accomplish this.

 

We feel that the Newsletter is based on, and aims provide an outlet for, the 2 Cs of ‘Creativity’ and ‘Collaboration’. We leave you with the following quotes indicate what we feel the newsletter can offer all of you:

“Creativity is just connecting things. When you ask creative people how they did something, they feel a little guilty because they didn’t really do it, they just saw something. It seemed obvious to them after a while. That’s because they were able to connect experiences they’ve had and synthesize new things.” – Steve Jobs

“Collaboration is the best way to work. It's only way to work, really. Everyone's there because they have a set of skills to offer across the board.”  – Anthony Starr

 

We hope you enjoyed all our issues, and we look forward to working with, and delivering to, a lot more of you in the future.

 

Thanks and Regards,

The OBP Newsletter Editorial Team
